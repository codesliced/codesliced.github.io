Projects

  • Hospitally

    Hospitals charge drastically different amounts for the same procedure. Our team of four Dev Bootcamp apprentices combined the recently released Medicare chargemaster data with government measures of quality such as patient survey results, geocoded 3k hospital addresses, and mapped the results with Google Maps. We wanted to make the data more accessible to the public and promote a discussion of the burgeoning cost of healthcare in the US.

  • PhotoOverflow

    PhotoOverflow is a StackOverflow clone geared towards photographers who want to get helpful advice from other photographers. The web app was built using Ruby on Rails, CarrierWave, Amazon S3, Bootstrap, and memcache.
